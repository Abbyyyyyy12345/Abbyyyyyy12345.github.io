Drawing

Definition:

Drawing is a visual art that uses an instrument to mark paper or another two-dimensional surface.This instruments must be pencils,crayons,pens with links , brushes with paints, or combinations of these and in a modern times,we use our gadgets to do drawings such as tablets, laptops, and our mobile phone.

History :

Drawing is one of the oldest forms of human expression, with evidence for its existence preceding that of written communication Before the invetion invention of the written language, demonstrated by the production of cave and rock paintings around 30,000 years ago (Art of the Upper Paleolithic).
